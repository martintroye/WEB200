Hannah Martin

Hannah Martin is passionate about volunteering and about educating both children and adults. From her time in the Peace Corps and other travels she has gained an understanding and appreciation for various cultures and people.

Experience

After college Hannah applied and was accepted into the Peace Corps. She was stationed in Benin Africa from June of 2016 to August of 2018. In Benin Hannah lived in a small village in the northern part of the country and taught the equivalent of middle school English. While in Benin she participated or organized several community projects such as: hand washing stations at the school, a mural of the globe painted on a school wall, English club, regional teacher training and sponsoring events to empower young women in the community.

Certifications

From her teaching experience in the Peace Corps Hannah earned her TEFL certification. TEFL stands for Teach English as a Foreign Language. I continue to teach adults English part-time through Metropolitan Community College.

Hobbies

In her spare time Hannah enjoys cooking, video games, reading and playing with the family dog Millie. Hannah also likes to run and has completed two half marathons. Besides Benin she has been fortunate to have visited several other countries including Spain, France, Togo and Ghana.
